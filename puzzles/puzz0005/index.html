Process Hints

The grids are all the same size


Look at the letter grid; in general, setters like to fill the letter grid with words, but in this case it is also hinting the solution


The first line of the letter grid is "OVERLAP"


Look at the overlap between the two solutions


Place that overlap in the letter grid



Solution
Shimaguni Puzzle Solutions

The shimaguni puzzle solutions are shown above


Using the fact that the first row of the letter grid says "OVERLAP", overlap the solutions of the two puzzles


Next part of solution

The first puzzle has been marked in red, the second in blue; any cell which is shaded in both is colored purple, a mix of red and blue, in the middle grid

Taking the purple cells, it spells out ARCHIPELAGO



Answer

ARCHIPELAGO
