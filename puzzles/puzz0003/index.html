Starting Idea

p(x) has unknown coefficients



Starting Action

Plug in the values and solve for the coefficients



Process Hints

Because there are seven values, the degree of the polynomial is most likely 7 - 1 = 6


Plugging in the values will create a 7x7 system of equations


You can either solve this yourself, or, using e.g. Wolfram|Alpha, have a computer solve for the coefficients


Depending on your age, the coefficients may be more or less familiar to you


If the numbers are unfamiliar to you, you are allowed to look them up



Solution

Solving for p(x), p(x) = 8 + 6x + 7x² + 5x³ + 3x⁴ + 0x⁵ + 9x⁶


"867-5309/Jenny" is a song by Tommy Tutone; thus, the answer is JENNY



Answer

JENNY
