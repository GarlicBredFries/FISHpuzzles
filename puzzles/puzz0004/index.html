Solution

The lines correspond to Joshua 4:11, Numbers 14:15, Deuteronomy 15:15, Genesis 19:14, Exodus 5:20, and Leviticus 3:2 in that order


In book order (Genesis, Exodus,...), the chapter:verse order is 19:14, 5:20, 3:2, 15:15, 14:15, and 4:11


Deciphering using A1Z26, you get S:N, E:T, C:B, O:O, N:O, D:K


Splitting between chapter and verse number, you get SECOND NTBOOK


The SECOND NT (New Testament) BOOK is MARK



Answer

MARK
