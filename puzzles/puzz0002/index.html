Starting Idea

These squares are in a familiar layout. Try looking around



Starting Action

Look at your keyboard



Process

The numbers are on certain keys


Place the letters in number order


There is a name for the French layout of keyboards



Solution

The keys in number order spell FRENCHLAYOUT


The FRENCH LAYOUT of keyboards is known as AZERTY



Answer
